ðŸš— Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Register" on the homepage, fill in your details including vehicle number, select your city and preferred parking lot, then submit the form.

How do I book a parking spot?

After logging in, go to your dashboard and click "Book a Spot". Select your preferred parking lot and the system will automatically assign you the first available spot.

Can I choose a specific parking spot?

No, the system automatically assigns the first available spot to ensure fair distribution and optimal space utilization.

I can't see car parks after selecting a city

After selecting a city, click the "Load Car Parks" button to see available parking locations in that city.

I forgot to release my parking spot

Go to your dashboard and click "Release Spot". The system will calculate the total cost based on your actual parking duration.

I can't find my booked parking spot

Check your dashboard for the spot number and parking lot details. The spot number is clearly displayed in your active booking information.
